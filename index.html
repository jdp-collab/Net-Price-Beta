Important Calculator Notice

Please review before using this tool.

This tool will provide general estimates of potential costs and financing options. It is accurate for students starting in September 2027 only and information is subject to change.

US Federal financial aid programs require you to be a U.S. citizen, U.S. national (including natives of American Samoa or Swain's Island), or permanent resident of the U.S. Other students are not eligible for US federal financial aid programmes and should not reference US Federal Aid information, which is presented for informational purposes for eligible students only.

You are NOT applying for financial aid when you complete this Calculator. To apply for financial aid, you should file the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and follow the instructions found on UCD's US Federal Aid page.

Financial aid estimates are based on full-time undergraduate enrollment and do not include merit-based scholarships or private scholarships from outside donors. Also, students using federal and/or state military benefits may have aid adjustments not captured in this estimate.

The information you provide and the estimated results you receive will not be stored by University College Dublin. You should print the results if you wish to retain a record.

⚠️ Please note: The estimate generated by the calculator is for informational purposes only. It does not represent a final determination, or actual offer, of financial assistance. It is not binding and may not reflect all recent updates to US federal loan programmes.

How to Use This Calculator

This Calculator is designed for informational purposes for full degree undergraduate and graduate taught Non-EU Fee Eligible students. Cost and aid figures are estimates and are based on current fee and cost data, US Department of Education regulations, and recently available advice for students starting in September 2027.

If you have questions about the information provided in this calculator, studying at UCD, financing your studies, or next steps please reach out at https://www.ucd.ie/global/enquire/
